Research

The solutions hub solutions platform was established, and it is the first online platform that introduces the concept of the market to the market of legal - accounting - engineering - shipping and transportation - trade - and financial services on the Internet in the Middle East. Our platform is region specific and supports a unique set of features

Strategy

• Multilingual support;
• Hiding the identity of customers;
• Verifying the licenses of service providers;
• A two-level control system for service providers;
• Several channels of communication between service providers and the customer;
• Local legal, accounting, engineering, logistical, commercial and financial information base

Design

• A directory of service providers facilitates the process of searching for service providers, comparing companies, and communicating with them.
• Free initial advice and assistance for service providers through the general inquiries and answers page, live chat, or requesting a call from the service provider;
• The service providers participating in the site offer some fixed-fee services that can be paid online: from consulting sessions, drafting and reviewing legal documents, establishing companies, and much more, the value of which can be determined in advance;
• A wide range of legal, accounting, engineering, logistical, commercial and financial information for the region, including: a database of legal, accounting, engineering, logistical, commercial and financial information, blog articles and a dictionary, and service providers’ answers to similar questions.

About

The solutions hub solutions hub is an online marketplace for legal, accounting, engineering, shipping and transportation, trade, and financial services that connects clients and service providers from various companies throughout the region. The solutions hub solutions platform is a company that provides technological support. We are not a law firm, accounting audit, engineering consultancy, shipping and transportation, trade, or financial services, and we do not provide consultations in those mentioned specialties. All consultations and services are provided by qualified and certified participating service providers.

The resolutions hub platform provides the following services to service providers:
• Effective platform to expand business and extend customer base;
• A commercial platform for purchasing services online;
• An information base in various fields that serves the scope of work of each service provider.
